About

Md. Jobayer Mia has graduated from the Department of Naval Architecture and Offshore Engineering, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman Maritime University, Bangladesh. He is passionate about Engineering, Creative thinking, Entrepreneurship, and Community Services. His research interests include Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Offshore Structure Simulation, Blue Economy, Ship Design and Performance, Maritime Safety, and Renewable Energy. He has worked on projects related to Flow Simulation, Structural Analysis, and Maritime Safety in Bangladesh. He has published research articles in well-known journals at home and abroad. He has a strong commitment to teamwork and the capacity to handle challenging situations effectively.
